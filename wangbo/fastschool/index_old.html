FastSchool English Instructor
Job Description

Who We Are

FastSchool is an online education company based in Beijing China, focusing on teaching Chinese students English through a brand new way. The company was founded by Mr. Fang Gang, the former senior VP of SOHU.COM (a Nasdaq listed company). Our online school offers personalized English Channels with experienced ESL teachers as well as youtubers who are all native English speakers. FastSchool guarantees to offer an interesting, practical and fun learning experience while helping students to achieve fluency in English.

About You

We are looking for fun and open-minded individuals. The following are some additional qualifications.
• TESOL/TEFL/CELTA certificate required
• Three or more years teaching experience preferred
• Must be fun, easy-going, and friendly to children
• Need to be willing to do extra work to prepare for every lesson
• Native English speakers, American/Canadian citizenship required
• At least a bachelor's degree in any field
• Has fast and stable Internet for online lessons
• Capable of managing an online kids talk show/lesson about culture and different subjects
• Punctual with a great sense of responsibility
• Available from 7pm to 9pm Beijing time, Monday through Sunday (All channels are open during this time, but you have freedom of choosing how many hours you want to work with us per week)
• Has no previous criminal records
• Has a well-functioning PC laptop or desktop

What We Offer

• 40 USD to 120 USD per hour (based on both performance and popularity in class)
• Teacher training to help you get used to our platform
• Convenience of working from any location you want
• Transparency between company senior leaders and teachers
• Possibility of becoming our Star Teacher, Teacher Quality Control Specialist, or a member of our staff here in Beijing, China!
• If you are in Beijing now, we welcome you to visit our office.
